Update on My School Situation

Posted on April 19, 2025 at 10:50 PM by: Lans van Glabbeek

For everyone who's been wondering about what's going on with school here's the full explanation.

A lot of people know that I'm planning to change course, but I haven't really shared any details with anyone. I've been getting quite a few questions lately, so I figured it's time to open up.

The truth is, I'm planning to leave my current school. I just don't fit into the system, and it's been wearing me down. I've never felt this stressed, and at the same time, so angry. It's affected me in ways I didn't expect I haven't been myself lately. I've been rude to friends, even to the point of cutting off contact with some people, and I've picked up some bad habits..

So I've decided it's time for a big change.

My current plan is to start a BBL study in the Netherlands. That means I'll be working four days a week as a junior software developer/engineer for a company, and going to school just one day a week. It sounds like the perfect setup for me: learning on the job, doing something I'm passionate about, and finally escaping a school system that doesn't work for me.

But it also comes with big sacrifices.

I'll be leaving my current school behind, and that means saying goodbye for now to the people I've known for years. I'll also have less contact with people my age, which is scary in itself. And the biggest change of all I'll be living with my grandfather during the week, only coming home on weekends. That part terrifies me, if I'm honest.


I believe that in the end everything falls into place even if it takes some time. And on that note I'll leave it here.
